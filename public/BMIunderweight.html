Tips to Overcome Underweight



  • Eat more calories

    • You need to consume more calories than you burn in order to gain weight. Increase your calorie intake by eating more nutrient-dense foods such as lean proteins, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates.



  • Exercise

    • Resistance training can help build muscle mass, which can contribute to weight gain. Try to incorporate strength training exercises into your workout routine.



  • Increase protein intake

    • Consuming adequate amounts of protein is essential for muscle growth and repair. Aim to consume at least 1 gram of protein per pound of body weight.



  • Eat frequently

    • Instead of consuming three large meals, try to eat several smaller meals throughout the day. This can help increase your calorie intake and promote weight gain.



  • Drink plenty of water

    • Staying hydrated is important for overall health and can help increase appetite.



  • Get enough sleep

    • Lack of sleep can lead to weight loss and can also affect your appetite. Aim to get at least 7-8 hours of sleep per night.



  • Reduce stress

    • Chronic stress can affect your appetite and contribute to weight loss. Try to manage stress through relaxation techniques such as meditation or yoga.
